Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the 'Forgot Password' link. Follow the instructions sent to your email to create a new password.
To add a new property, navigate to the Properties page and click on the 'Create New Property' button. Fill in all the required details and submit the form.
If a customer complains about a property, listen to their concerns and document the issue. Then, investigate the problem and work with the property owner to resolve it. Keep the customer updated throughout the process.
To process a refund, go to the Bookings page, find the specific booking, and click on the 'Refund' option. Follow the prompts to complete the refund process. Make sure to communicate with the customer about the refund status.
