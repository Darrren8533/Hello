Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

To book a tour, navigate to our Tour page, select your desired tour, and follow the booking instructions. If you need assistance, please contact our support team.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the tour and how far in advance you cancel. Please refer to the specific tour details or contact our support team for more information.

Are your tours suitable for children?

Many of our tours are family-friendly, but some may have age restrictions. Check the tour details or contact us for specific information about child suitability and any discounts available for children.

What should I bring on my tour?

The items you should bring depend on the specific tour. Generally, we recommend comfortable clothing, walking shoes, sunscreen, and a camera. Detailed packing lists are provided for each tour upon booking.
