About Hello Sarawak

Hello Sarawak is your gateway to the enchanting world of Sarawak, Malaysia's largest state on the island of Borneo. Our mission is to showcase the rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and unique experiences that Sarawak has to offer.

Founded in 2020, our team of passionate locals and travel experts have come together to create unforgettable journeys for visitors from around the globe. We believe in sustainable tourism that benefits local communities while preserving the natural environment.

From the vibrant streets of Kuching to the lush rainforests of Gunung Mulu National Park, we curate experiences that capture the essence of Sarawak. Whether you're seeking adventure, cultural immersion, or simply a peaceful getaway, Hello Sarawak is here to make your journey truly memorable.
